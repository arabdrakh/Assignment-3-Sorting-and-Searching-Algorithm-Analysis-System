????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
?                          KEY OBSERVATIONS                                    ?
????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
?  1. Insertion Sort is fast on small/sorted data but degrades on large        ?
?     random arrays due to O(n▓) complexity.                                   ?
?                                                                              ?
?  2. Merge Sort maintains consistent O(n log n) performance regardless        ?
?     of input order, making it superior for large datasets.                   ?
?                                                                              ?
?  3. Binary Search achieves O(log n) ù extremely fast, but REQUIRES           ?
?     a sorted array to function correctly.                                    ?
?                                                                              ?
?  4. On sorted input, Insertion Sort's best-case O(n) can outperform          ?
?     Merge Sort's O(n log n) because of lower constant overhead.              ?
